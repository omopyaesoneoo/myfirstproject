Anawrahta Minsaw

Anawrahta Minsaw (Burmese: အနော်ရထာ မင်းစော, pronounced ; 11 May 1014 – 11 April 1077) was the founder of the Bagan Empire.

Ledi Sayadaw U Ñaṇadhaja

Ledi Sayadaw U Ñaṇadhaja (Burmese: လယ်တီဆရာတော် ဦးဉာဏဓဇ, pronounced ; 1 December 1846 – 27 June 1923) was an influential Theravada Buddhist monk.

Theravāda Buddha Sāsana

Buddhism (Burmese: ဗုဒ္ဓဘာသာ), specifically Theravāda Buddhism (Burmese: ထေရဝါဒဗုဒ္ဓဘာသာ), is the State religion of Myanmar since 1961, and practiced by nearly 90% of the population.
